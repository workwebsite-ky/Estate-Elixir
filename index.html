FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my package after purchasing?
Absolutely. You can upgrade to a higher-tier package at any point by paying the difference. Our team will seamlessly transition your service level without any disruption to your property search or transaction.
Is the consultation fee refundable?
The package fee is fully credited toward your transaction commission if you proceed with a purchase or sale through Estate Elixir. We believe in rewarding commitment and loyalty.
Do you offer custom packages for investors?
Yes. For investors with portfolios of 3+ properties or transactions exceeding $10M, we craft bespoke advisory agreements. Contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and we'll design a program that fits perfectly.
How long does the buying process typically take?
Timelines vary depending on location, property type, and financing. Typically, a standard residential purchase takes 8–12 weeks from offer acceptance. Our team actively expedites every step to minimize delays and ensure a smooth closing.
Do you assist with international property purchases?
Yes, our Elite package includes access to our global network spanning 40+ cities. We partner with trusted local agents and legal firms in each jurisdiction to ensure compliant, stress-free international acquisitions.
